Troy Barnes

AKA T-Bone, Butt Soup

Favorite Football Player: Troy Barnes

Troy Barnes (played by Donald Glover) is a graduate from both Riverside High School and Greendale Community College. A popular figure at Riverside, Troy was the star quarterback of the football team and also the prom king. His many admirers at school included his fellow classmate Annie Edison who would later join him at Greendale and become his roommate.

In his younger years he lived it up, marrying seven times and accumulating over thirty step-children.
